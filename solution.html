Our Solution

  • SenseRem is our pocket IoT solution, assessing your vitals remotely at an affordable cost
  • SenseRem is a non-invasive medical device, co-developed with physicians and chronic-ill patients.
  • SenseRem capture with accuracy the following data all-in-one :
  • SPo2 : To check oxygen saturation in blood SPo2 is an indicator of imbalances such as anemia, COPD or a stroke
  • ECG : To highlight heart electrical activity, ECG is a tool helping diagnosing conditions like arrhythmia, strokes or an ischemia
  • Inner sound : To assess lung, abdominal and vascular homeostasis
  • Blood pressure : To monitor arterial tension, and prevent heart diseases or strokes
  • Temperature : To detect fever or cold events
  • Skin condition : To spot dermatological diseases symptoms (alopecia, acne, prurit, vitiligo...)
